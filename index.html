How to Play
The Rules
1
Players take turns counting from 1 to 10, one number per turn in order.
2
When a player lands on number 10, they must assign a secret word to any unassigned number of their choice.
3
After assigning, counting restarts from 1 with the next player.
4
Whenever an assigned number comes up, say the word instead of the number. Judge clicks ✓ or ✗.
5
You have 30 seconds per turn. Forget the word or time out → you're OUT!
6
Last player standing wins! Eliminated players' words stay in the game.
